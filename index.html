About

Human-centered systems with real operational depth.

The strongest HR work is not only efficient. It is also trusted, clear, and deeply aware of how people actually experience the workplace.

Puja is an HR Professional who believes that people thrive when they feel heard, supported, and valued. With 4+ years of experience across Talent Acquisition, HR Operations, and Employee Relations, she has built her career around creating workplaces where individuals can grow confidently and teams can perform with purpose.

Her approach is simple: understand people deeply, communicate openly, and design HR processes that feel human, not transactional.

Puja has helped diverse teams find the right talent, navigate challenges, and build a culture of trust, clarity, and connection. She values empathy just as much as efficiency, ensuring that every decision contributes to a healthier and more motivated workplace.
